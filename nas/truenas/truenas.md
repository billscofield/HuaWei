TrueNAS Minimum Requirements

    1. 64-Bit CPU
    2. 16 GB of RAM Memory
    3. 16 GB Boot Drive (SSD Encouraged)
    4. At Least One Attached Disk
    5. Network Port
    6. Hardware RAID Not Required
